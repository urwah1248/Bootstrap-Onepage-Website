About Us

ENCE was originally founded in 2013 to professionalize the Finnish esports landscape. Our mission has always been to build a sustainable winning culture and know-how of creating success stories.

With the rise of esports towards the mainstream in the last few years, we have also strived to enable a vocal fanbase to be heard and play a part in everything ENCE does.
